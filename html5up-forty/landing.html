My service

I ensured that all projects with my clients will be completed and delivered on time. To meet our goals and visions, it is essential we work together and communicate effectively. My model plans for success:

  1. Consultation- Let's pinpoint the ideal website together through any form of conversations or meetings.
  2. Proposals- Follow up after the initial consultation with a few drafts of our potential website.
  3. Development Updates- Clients will receive weekly updates about their website development while I continue to add more it.
  4. Website Launch - Once the project is completed, clients will have my support to help launching their websites. I am also open to provide ongoing support to the completed websites if needed.
